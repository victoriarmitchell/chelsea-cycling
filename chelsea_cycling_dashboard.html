At the current FTP trajectory (~7W per 6-week block), Chelsea reaches ~190W by the October CX season start — the low end of Cat 1 competitive range. The bigger lever between now and October is structured anaerobic work: CX races are won by repeated accelerations above FTP, not sustained power. Adding 2–3 weekly sessions of short Z5/Z6 intervals (30/30s, over-unders, hill reps) in the next block will close the Cat 2 gap faster than continued endurance volume alone.
